Kanya Qwalela - Youth Fellowship Leader

"Empowering Young Lives for Christ"

Our Youth Fellowship at Divine Life Restoration Centre is a vibrant community of young people dedicated to growing in faith, building meaningful relationships, and deepening their knowledge of Christ.

Mission:

To create a safe and supportive environment where youth can deepen their relationship with God, develop leadership skills, and serve the church.

Activities:

  • Weekly meetings on Saturdays for Bible study, games, and discussions
  • Monthly outreach events like Street Evangelism
  • Small groups for mentorship and accountability
  • Special events such as Pyjama Night for Girls and Movie Night for all youth and teenagers
  • Bible studies and recreational games throughout the year

Goals:

  • To help youth develop a strong foundation in their faith
  • To provide a supportive community for spiritual growth and encouragement
  • To equip youth with leadership skills and opportunities for service
  • To foster meaningful relationships and friendships

Let's build a strong and vibrant youth community that honors God and makes a difference in the world.

Prophet L. Mahashe (White Horse)

Prophet L. Mahashe was ministering and preaching to the youth and everyone on where God should raise defenders of the Gospel. His powerful message encouraged the youth to stand firm in their faith and become bold advocates for Christ in their communities.

Pastor L. Tsengiwe

Pastor L. Tsengiwe also preached and addressed the youth and everyone, emphasizing the importance of living a life dedicated to Christ. His inspiring words motivated the youth to pursue a deeper relationship with God and to serve others with love and compassion.

Career Exhibition & Graduation Celebration

During the Youth Seminar 2024, we hosted an inspiring Career Exhibition and Graduation Celebration, honoring both recent graduates and established professionals. This event provided a platform for networking, mentorship, and career guidance.

Young graduates were celebrated for their achievements and encouraged to pursue their career aspirations with faith and diligence. Seasoned professionals shared their journeys and insights, inspiring the youth to excel in their chosen fields while remaining steadfast in their faith.

The exhibition featured interactive booths, workshops, and panel discussions covering various industries, from business and technology to healthcare and arts. It was a vibrant display of talent, ambition, and God's guidance in shaping future leaders.

Inspirational Guest Speakers

We were honored to have four distinguished guest speakers who shared their wisdom and insights on the importance of education, inspiring both young people and graduates to pursue their academic and career goals with determination and faith.

Mrs. Godana - Department of Education

Mrs. Godana emphasized the critical role of education in personal and community development. She encouraged the youth to strive for excellence in their studies and to see education as a powerful tool for change. Her insights underscored the value of lifelong learning and perseverance.

Mrs. Mpongoshe - Ikhala TVET College

Mrs. Mpongoshe highlighted the opportunities available through technical and vocational education. She shared success stories of students who transformed their lives through skills training and urged the youth to explore diverse educational pathways. Her message was one of hope and practical action.

Mrs. Mosoang - Walter Sisulu University

Mrs. Mosoang spoke about the transformative power of higher education. She encouraged students to embrace university life with enthusiasm and to leverage the resources available for their growth. Her words inspired a vision of academic success and community impact.

Miss Nangamso Anam Nganeka

Miss Nangamso Anam Nganeka delivered a passionate speech on the importance of resilience and hard work in achieving educational goals. She shared her personal journey and motivated the youth to pursue their dreams despite challenges. Her story was a testament to determination and faith.
