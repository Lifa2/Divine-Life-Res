Welcome to Divine Life Restoration Centre

Experience The Power of Divine Love and Grace

Our History

Divine Life Restoration Centre was founded in 2019 with the mission to restore lives through the power of divine love and grace. Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spiritual growth, service, and fellowship.

Under the leadership of our esteemed Bishop and Lady Bishop Mngxaso assistance, we have expanded our outreach programs and established numerous ministries to cater to the diverse needs of our congregation and the community at large.

Join us as we continue to build a legacy of faith, hope, and love.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread the message of divine restoration through worship, teaching, and community service. We are committed to empowering individuals to live their best lives through faith and the guidance of the Holy Spirit.

Join us in our mission to bring hope and healing to our community and beyond.

Central to our mission is the belief that every person is created with a unique purpose and potential. Through our various ministries, we aim to provide the tools and support necessary for individuals to discover and fulfill their God-given destinies. We emphasize spiritual growth, personal development, and the cultivation of a strong moral foundation.

Our worship services are designed to be a vibrant expression of our faith, where people can connect with God in a meaningful way. Through heartfelt praise, inspiring messages, and an atmosphere of reverence, we create a space for spiritual renewal and transformation. Our goal is to inspire a deep, lasting relationship with God that influences every aspect of life.

Our Church Overview

Divine Life Restoration Centre was established in 2019 under the visionary leadership of Bishop CA Mngxaso and Lady Bishop Mngxaso. Our church began as a small congregation in Whittlesea and has since grown to multiple branches across the region, including Queenstown, Thornhill, and Lady Frere. Our mission is to restore lives through the power of divine love and grace, and we are committed to impacting our community through faith-based initiatives and outreach programs.

Our church is a place where individuals and families can experience spiritual growth, support, and fellowship. We believe in creating an inclusive environment where everyone is welcome, regardless of their background. Our vibrant worship services, dynamic teachings, and various ministries cater to the diverse needs of our congregation and the broader community. In addition to our spiritual programs, we are deeply committed to social responsibility and community service. Our outreach initiatives include feeding programs, educational support, and healthcare services, aimed at addressing the needs of the less fortunate in our society.

We place a high value on family and youth development, offering a range of programs for children, teenagers, and young adults. Through our youth fellowship and various activities, we aim to nurture the next generation of leaders who will carry forward the vision and mission of our church. We invite you to join us on this journey of faith, hope, and love. Whether you are seeking spiritual guidance, looking for a community to belong to, or wanting to make a difference in the world, Divine Life Restoration Centre is the place for you.

Our Background

The Divine Life Restoration Centre (DLRC) is an embodiment of faith, resilience, and community spirit. Founded in 2019 by Bishop CA Mngxaso and Lady Bishop Mngxaso, the church was established with a clear vision: to restore lives through the power of divine love and grace. The journey of DLRC began in Whittlesea, a small yet vibrant town, where the first branch was planted. From these humble beginnings, DLRC has grown into a multi-branch ministry, touching lives and spreading the gospel across Queenstown, Thornhill, and Lady Frere.

Our church is a beacon of hope and a sanctuary for all who seek spiritual nourishment and community. The leadership of Bishop and Lady Bishop Mngxaso has been instrumental in guiding the church through its formative years, fostering a culture of love, acceptance, and spiritual growth. Their unwavering commitment to the mission of DLRC has inspired many to join and support the vision, leading to the establishment of various ministries that cater to the diverse needs of our congregation.

Today, Divine Life Restoration Centre stands as a testament to the transformative power of faith. Our church continues to thrive, driven by the collective efforts of our members and leaders who are dedicated to making a positive impact in their communities. We invite you to become a part of our story, to experience the warmth of our fellowship, and to grow with us in faith and purpose.
